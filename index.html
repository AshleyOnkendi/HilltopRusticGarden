Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance for the Koroga experience?

Yes, we highly recommend calling us to book your space and your mbuzi in advance, especially during the weekends when we get very busy!

Is there secure parking available?

Yes, we have ample and highly secure parking on-site for all our guests at no extra cost.

Are children allowed?

Absolutely! Hilltop Rustic Garden is a family-friendly establishment. Our lush green gardens offer plenty of safe outdoor space for kids to play.

Do you offer food delivery?

Yes! We offer FREE delivery for all food and drink orders within Limuru town. Just give us a call to place your order.

Can I host a private event or birthday party here?

Definitely! We specialize in outdoor catering for parties, birthdays, team building, and squad hangouts. Reach out to us to reserve your date and discuss the menu!
