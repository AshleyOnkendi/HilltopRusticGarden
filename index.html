A Welcoming Getaway

Located in the cool tea highlands of Limuru, Hilltop Getaway Grill is a peaceful escape from the busy city. We don’t just serve food… we serve EXPERIENCE. Food lovers, this place is dangerous… you’ll keep coming back.
